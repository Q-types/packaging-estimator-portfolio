How to Use

  1. Enter a Job Name to identify your estimate
  2. Specify the Quantity of items needed
  3. Enter the Dimensions of your packaging in millimetres
  4. Select Materials - board type, outer wrap, and liner
  5. Check the Operations required for production
  6. Toggle Rush Order if you need expedited delivery
  7. Click Calculate Estimate to get your quote
